Zeyu is a PhD student on the Tri-Institutional Programme in Computational Biology and Medicine.
He previously earned a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Molecular Engineering from
Imperial College London, where he did research in synthetic and systems biology,
as well as AI for medical imaging. After graduating, he continued his research at
Stanford University focusing on spatial omics analysis. Outside the lab, Zeyu enjoys
football :soccer:️, bouldering, and seeking out the best afternoon teas in NYC.
